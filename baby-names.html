6.1 Bens over time

babynames |>
  filter(name %in% c("Ben", "Benjamin", "Benji")) |> 
  group_by(year, sex) |> 
  summarize(total_ben = sum(n)) |> 
  ggplot() +
  geom_line(aes(x = year, y = total_ben)) +
  facet_wrap(~sex)
## `summarise()` has grouped output by 'year'. You can override using
## the `.groups` argument.
